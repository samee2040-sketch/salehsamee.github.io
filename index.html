Saleh Ahmed Samee

Session Guitarist • Multi-Instrumentalist • Music Producer

About

Saleh Ahmed Samee, professionally known as Saleh Samee, is a Bangladeshi guitarist, multi-instrumentalist, and composer celebrated for blending contemporary pop, rock, and electronic sounds with the rich heritage of ethnic string instruments. With extensive work as a session guitarist, composer, and producer, he is a sought-after collaborator in Bangladesh's mainstream industry and international music circuits. He is widely recognized for energetic live performances and his iconic Pink Panther custom Stratocaster.

Instruments & Production

As a multi-instrumentalist and producer, Saleh specializes in complex string layering and high-end audio production using Logic Pro.

Electric Guitar Afghan Rabab Mandolin Dotara Saz Oud Logic Pro
