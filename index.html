Saleh Ahmed Samee

About

Saleh Ahmed Samee, professionally known as Saleh Samee, is a Bangladeshi guitarist, multi-instrumentalist, and composer. He is celebrated for blending contemporary pop, rock, and electronic sounds with ethnic string instruments. Widely recognized for his work with Habib Wahid and Coke Studio Bangla, he is a sought-after collaborator in the international music circuit.
