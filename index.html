Saleh Samee

Multi-Instrumentalist • Guitarist • Composer

About

Saleh Samee is a Dhaka-based musician known for fusing modern soundscapes with ethnic string instruments like the Afghan Rubab and Mandolin. He is recognized for his signature Pink Panther Stratocaster and his work with Habib Wahid and Coke Studio Bangla.

Notable Credits

  • Coke Studio Bangla (S3): Featured Rubab Artist on "Moha Jadu"
  • Habib Wahid (2021-2025): Lead Guitarist (USA, Australia, Riyadh Tours)
  • National Honors: Solo Mandolinist at Shilpakala Academy (2022)
