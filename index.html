Saleh Samee

Multi-Instrumentalist • Guitarist • Composer

About

Saleh Samee is a Dhaka-based musician building a distinct voice between tradition and modernity. As a sought-after session player, composer, and multi-instrumentalist, he seamlessly integrates ethnic string instruments—including the Afghan Rubab, Mandolin, Dotara, Oud, and Saz—into contemporary pop, rock, and electronic arrangements.

Recognizable by his signature customized Pink Panther Stratocaster, his work is guided by precision, adaptability, and a deep engagement with cross-cultural sonic identities.

Notable Credits & Performances

  • Coke Studio Bangla (Season 3): Featured Rubab artist on the track "Moha Jadu".
  • Habib Wahid (2021-2025): Full-time dedicated guitarist, performing on major international stages including Riyadh Season (Saudi Arabia), and extensive tours across the USA and Australia.
  • IPDC Amader Gaan / Joler Gaan: Guest Mandolin player for the acclaimed track "Shua Urilo".
  • National Instrumental Music Festival (2022): Solo Mandolin performance broadcast live from the Bangladesh Shilpakala Academy.
  • Compositions & Studio Work: Contributed to the film Damal (Dotara), national branding campaigns (Grameenphone, Aarong), and composed hit original tracks like "Keu Nei" for Tasrif Khan.
